It seems that the Reimu of this world was not able to perfect her Fantasy Seal Spell Card before the incident occurred. It appears that this Fantasy Seal incorporates aspects similar to the Fantasy Nature Last Word due to its experimental nature and this Reimu's inexperience, making it in some ways more powerful than the perfected version.
